Projects

The Customer Recovery and Liability department needed a deeper understanding of the claims, complaitnts and compliments submitted to SAS. The only data available previously were the current queue status.

This interactive tool gives the them the possibility to look at several corralations, analyze trends and follow up on categories, teams or sites. The dashboard uses Microsoft's Power Bi and data is exctracted and processed from the database with SQL. All visuals are interactive and connected to each other with create endless possibilities to explore the data.

Some exampleas of what can be viewed in the dasboard are:

  • Corralation between the amount of working agents and closed cases.
  • More in depth follow up on productivity, eg. the amount of emails sent, amount of expected and unexpected return emails from customers.
  • Better understanding of the KPIs.
  • Further information of the queue status.

With the help of this dashboard has the team changed a some routines for how to handle cases. The most significan is that they now primarly focuses on cases in the queue instead of return emails. This was changed after we discovered that a case can ususally be slolved with one email to the customer (our decision/reply) and only 10% of the cases that has been marked as closed by an agent are reopened again.
